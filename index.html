NervesConf

October 11, 2021

Austin, Texas

Register Now

NervesConf 2021 is a one day conference, featuring 5 speakers, and interspersed with hands-on training. Conference admission includes the Raspberry Pi and sensors you'll use for the training.

The conference is being held at the Norris Conference Center, the same venue as ElixirConf, from 12:00pm to 5:00pm on the day before ElixirConf.

ElixirConf is known for great Nerves content. We are working with the organizers of ElixirConf to ensure there is no overlap in speakers. NervesConf is aiming to give Nerves talks at all levels.

Training

In between speakers, we will be working through some Nerves projects. Your admission includes all of the hardware you'll need to complete the trainings - and it's yours to keep.
